About my Photography:

This photography webpage is a highlight reel consisting of my favorite photos from my time as an amateur photographer. All these photos were taken using a Fujifilm Finepix S9200 bridge camera. I hope you enjoy looking through them as much as I enjoyed taking them.

Exposure Triangle Poster

This is a poster I made for my Computer Engineering Technical Writing course. It is a poster detailing the three most important settings when taking a picture. The ISO, aperture, and shutter speed settings all determine a picture's exposure.
